## About 27tools
27tools started as a simple idea. Build small tools that actually make life easier.

No unnecessary steps. No overcomplicated interfaces.

Just tools that do what they say, quickly and clearly.

## The thinking behind it
There are thousands of tools online, but most of them:

- Feel bloated
- Are filled with distractions
- Or just do not work that well

27tools is about fixing that.

Each tool is designed to be:

- Fast
- Simple
- Easy to use
- Actually useful

If something can be done in one step, it should be.

## Built by one person
27tools is an independent project, built alongside other work, slowly, carefully, and with a focus on getting things right.

Every tool is something I have either needed myself or know people will use regularly.

## The goal
To build a collection of tools people genuinely rely on.

If a tool saves you time, even once, it has done its job.
